Mulch Masters

Turning discarded tires into durable, eco-friendly mulch that reduces waste, improves landscaping performance, and creates long-term value.

The Problem

Every year, over 300 million tires are discarded in the United States alone. These tires do not decompose easily and can create major environmental and public health hazards, including toxic runoff, mosquito breeding grounds, and long-lasting tire fires.

Our Solution

Mulch Masters Recycling converts old, unusable tires into tire mulch, a durable and eco-friendly alternative to traditional wood mulch.

Collect

Gather discarded tires from tire shops, auto businesses, and municipalities.

Process

Shred and clean the tires into safe, long-lasting mulch material.

Distribute

Supply the finished product to landscapers, schools, parks, and homeowners.

Target Market

Landscaping companies
Municipal parks and recreation departments
Schools and playground developers
Environmentally conscious homeowners

Competitive Advantage

Steady Supply

Direct partnerships with tire retailers and auto shops create reliable, low-cost input.

Regional Focus

Operating locally reduces transportation costs and emissions.

Long-Term Value

Tire mulch lasts much longer than wood mulch, reducing replacement costs.

Sustainability

We turn waste into a valuable product and support a circular economy.
